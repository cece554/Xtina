Christina starr

Xtina Starr /Christina Starr/, is a House DJ specializing in sounds of the African Diaspora and a Senior Engineering Manager at Leafly making cannabis more accessible. Based between Dallas & Brooklyn.

Engineer by day

An emblem of curiosity and exploration, Xtina Starr loves an adventure and takes pleasure in taking the crowd on a healing sonic journey. Inspired by the sounds throughout the continent of Africa, she especially pulls rhythms from the southern region and is one of the ‘go-to’ tastemakers for Afro House and Amapiano in New York City, and was early in introducing the city to these captivating genres. Xtina Starr aims to raise the vibration of the people, one track at a time.
